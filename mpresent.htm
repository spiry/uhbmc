MARVEL COMICS PRESENTS

Marvel Comics Presents (I) (1988-1995)

The covers of issues #1 to #89 are either a double-spread page or there is a front- and a backcover. The covers feature the characters from the issue. Issues #90 to #175 (except #100, 150 and 151) are "flip books" with two front covers (of which we treat one as back cover). There are only the main characters on the cover. Issues #150 and #151 contain only one story and double-spread covers with all characters.
