UPC: 7-59606-06637-7

To celebrate the #600 issue, the numbering started with Tales to Astonish (I) #1 and later continued in The Incredible Hulk (II) #102 is resumed with the issue dated September 2009. However, there is an offset of 1 between the book number and the later published Legacy Number (most likely from counting Hulk (III) #13 or not).

The series is named "Incredible Hulks" from #612 on.

Hulk story continued from Hulk (III) #12.

Skaar story continued from Skaar: Son of Hulk #12.

Legacy Numbering continued from Hulk (III) #12.
