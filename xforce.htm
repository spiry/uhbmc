X-FORCE

X-Force (I) (1991-2002)

UPC:7-59606-01766-9

Series was published with the "Counter-X" brand from #102 to #115.

Issue #1 was also sold poly-bagged with an X-Force T-shirt (white with a color picture of X-Force) and two cards (costing $15.95). One was an ad for the T-shirts, the other was a trading card with one of five motives (Team card, Cable card, Deadpool card, Sunspot & Gideon card and Shatterstar card). Some of the #1 covers show the "Captain America 50th Aniverssary logo" with white face and black background, others show it with black face and white background and on some (extremely rare) it is replaced by a bar code.

X-Force story continued from The New Mutants (I) #100.
