CAPTAIN AMERICA COMICS

Captain America Comics (Steve Rogers) • Captain America Weird Tales (1941-1954)

Series is named "Captain America's Weird Tales" from #74 to #75.

Lacking the original issues in my collection and even suitable reprints for most issues, I filled in missing data using the Avengers, Thor and Captain America: Official Index to the Marvel Universe. And who should know better who did what than Marvel themselves? On the other hand, to quote Marvel:
"At the time of Golden Age comic book production, it was not industry standard to provide detailed credits for each strip. It's possible these reprint stories were worked on by any number of artists or assistant artists who worked in the Simon & Kirby studio."
