Flood Relief (Malibu Comics) (1994)

Spartan Printing is proud to be participating in this flood relief project with Malibu Comics. We are donating the printing of this publication in order to benefit those of our neighbors affected by this disaster.
On behalf of all the members of Malibu Comics and the American Red Cross, we would like to thank the following for donating their time, energy, and advertising space towards this humanitarian project: Andromeda Distribution, Arena Magazine, Capital City Distribution, Comic Book Collector, Comic Buyers Guide, Comics Retailer, Comics Scene, Comics Value Monthly, Diamond Comic Distributors, Friendly Franks Distribution, Gary Colabuono - Moon Dogs, Hero Illustrated, Heroes World Distribution, Lee Tennant Enterprises, Multi-Book Distribution, Shelton Drum - Heroes Aren't Hard to find, Styx Distribution, Wizard.
