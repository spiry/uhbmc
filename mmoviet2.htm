Blade: Nightstalking (DVD Special) (2005)

Exclusive comic created specifically for the Blade: Trinity DVD.
While marketed as a Blade comic, he only appears in a half-page flashback. The comic is about Abigail meeting Whistler, then her first meeting with Hannibal King and the forming of Nightstalkers. (Basically, because Abigail is a Rachel Van Helsing knockoff, this story gives her a Marvel origin and some background info needed to fully enjoy the movie).
