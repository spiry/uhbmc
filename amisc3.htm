ACTOR Comics Presents (Hero Initiative) (2006)

Announced in Marvel Previews (I) #2 as "A.C.T.O.R. Presents Spider-Man and the Incredible Hulk" and said to have two stories by Bill Messner-Loebs and Ron Marz (writers) and Dave Simons and Dan Jurgens (pencilers) with a cover by Joseph Michael Linsner. Proceeds were planned to benefit A.C.T.O.R., the charitable orgianization dedicated to provide financial assistance to disadvantaged comic book creators. The issue was canceled, but finally published in fall 2006 by Century Comics for the ACTOR-HERO Initiative.
