All(*) theses issues feature:

  • The corner box shows a picture of Spider-Man by John Romita and has the text "Marvel Remembers John Romita".
  • The inner front cover page has a picture of Romita surrounded with characters from his run on The Amazing Spider-Man (I) by Romita himself.
  • A drawing of Romita by ?, a photo of him and a short obituary.
  • Two pages of art by Romita (including the covers of The Amazing Spider-Man (I) #50 "Spider-Man no more!" and The Amazing Spider-Man Annual (I) #21 "Wedding special") with personal notes by Kevin Feige, C.B. Cebulski, Nick Lowe and Dan Buckley.

(*) A few of the issues do not have the corner box, but still have the remaining features.
