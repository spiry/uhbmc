The cover of each issue features the main characters described in that issue. The covers of issues #1-12 link together to one big picture, 3 covers broad and 4 covers in height. Also, the covers of #13 and 14 link together to one big picture.

The cover of #1 says "No. 1 in a series of twelve", so it seems that the three "Books of the Dead" were originally not intented to be part of the series. (Gruenwald announces the expansion in #12.)
