AVENGERS

Avengers (Four Ltd Series) (2017)

UPC:7-59606-08537-8

The name of the series in the imprint and on the cover is "Avengers" only. However as there is currently another series published that is also named "Avengers", but has nothing to do with this series, I decided to to give this series a unique name according to the TPB in which the issues were collected.

The story of this limited series takes place between Avengers (I) #16 and #17.
