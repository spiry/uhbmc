Universal Product Codes

A list of UPCs

In the following you can find a list of the UPCs of series, sorted by the UPC. (At least as far as I could collect them so far.)

Marvel started to put UPCs with a checksum, the issue number and the cover number (adding up to 17 digits) on the covers of their comics in 1993/07. Many comics before that had product codes with eleven digits followed by two digits that were derived from the month of publication (at least it seems to be so). That resulted in the product code being repeated once every year. I did not collect all these old codes for that reason, just enough to identify the series.
